What Stokvel Pro is

Stokvel Pro is a coordination and clarity tool that helps stokvel members stay aligned on contributions, payout order, and group progress.

What Stokvel Pro is not

Stokvel Pro is not a bank, payment service, or financial institution. The app does not move, store, or control money on behalf of any group or member.

How contributions work

All contributions are made outside the app using methods agreed by the group (cash, EFT, mobile transfer, etc.). Stokvel Pro only shows a shared view of contribution status.

Payouts and rotation

Payout order and schedules are agreed by the group. Stokvel Pro displays this information so everyone can see the same plan. The app does not enforce or automate payouts.

Group responsibility

Each stokvel is responsible for agreeing on its own rules, ensuring information entered is accurate, and resolving disputes within the group.

Admin responsibility

Group admins are responsible for maintaining accurate records and updating contribution or payout status as agreed by members.
